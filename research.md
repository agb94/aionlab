AION Lab focuses on **analog/mixed-signal ICs, neural interface system ICs, physical AI sensor ICs, and process-in-memory**. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## 1) Neural Interface System IC
- High-density multi-channel neural recording ICs for next-generation neurotechnology
- Artifact-tolerant, wide-dynamic-range front-ends for closed-loop interfaces
- Recording/stimulation/closed-loop circuit architectures (incl. accepted CICC 2026 tutorial-style work) :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## 2) Data Converters for Bio/Sensor Readout
- ΔΣ ADC (CT/DT hybrid), NS-SAR, time-based readout architectures
- Dynamic-range enhancement and noise-shaping techniques for robust acquisition :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

## 3) Physical AI Sensor Interface IC
- Multimodal wireless biosignal sensor interfaces for wearable robotics
- Ultra-low-power sensing SoCs and interference-tolerant acquisition circuits :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## 4) Process-in-Memory / Compute-in-Memory
